Society Manager

Website

Society Manager is the website portal designed to reduce conflicts among society members. The system has automated functionality for calculating monthly maintenance and member can view their bill status on their account. The additional functionality of this project is that, member can rent their house on the portal and visitors can see which house is available for rent purpose and can directly contact to the owner of the house. Besides, Society Manager allows members to login with their own account and get updated with society happenings.
